## About Me

I am a PhD candidate at Hamburg Universität working on **magnetohydrodynamical simulations of galaxy clusters**. My research focuses on understanding the complex physics governing the intracluster medium, with a particular interest in multiphase gas dynamics.

When I'm not running simulations, you'll find me bouldering, playing the drums, attending concerts, or practicing photography.
